# 📸 Easy Image Upload Guide

## Upload Your Coffee Image and Other Product Images

---

## 🎯 Method: Admin Panel (Easiest & Recommended)

### Step 1: Prepare Your Coffee Image
1. **Save the coffee image** from your screenshot
2. Name it: `coffee.jpg` or `coffee.png`
3. Remember where you saved it

### Step 2: Access Admin Panel
1. **Start the server:**
   ```
   start_server.bat
   ```

2. **Open browser and go to:**
   ```
   http://127.0.0.1:8000/admin/
   ```

3. **Login:**
   - Username: `admin`
   - Password: `admin123`

### Step 3: Upload Coffee Image
1. Click **"Products"** (under PRODUCTS section)
2. Find **"Coffee Maker"** in the list
3. Click on **"Coffee Maker"**
4. Scroll down to find **"Image"** field
5. You'll see: **"Currently: products/coffee_maker.png"**
6. Click **"Choose File"** button (or "Browse")
7. Select your coffee image file
8. Click **"Save"** button at the bottom

### Step 4: View Your Coffee Image
1. Open new tab: `http://127.0.0.1:8000/products/`
2. Find Coffee Maker product
3. **You'll see your beautiful coffee image!** ☕

## 🖼️ Where to Get Product Images

### Free Stock Photo Websites:

1. **Unsplash** - https://unsplash.com/
   - Search: "coffee maker", "headphones", "smartwatch", etc.
   - High quality, free to use
   - No attribution required

2. **Pexels** - https://pexels.com/
   - Search product names
   - Free for commercial use
   - Great variety

3. **Pixabay** - https://pixabay.com/
   - Free images
   - No copyright issues
   - Easy download

### Search Tips:
- Add "white background" for clean images
- Add "product photo" for professional shots
- Example: "coffee maker white background"
- Example: "wireless headphones product photo"

## 💡 Image Requirements

### Best Practices:
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **Size:** 400x400 pixels or larger (square preferred)
- **File Size:** Under 5MB
- **Quality:** Clear and sharp
- **Background:** White or clean background
- **Product:** Centered and well-lit
