### 1. **View All Products** 📦
- See all 143 products in a grid
- View product images, names, prices
- Check stock status at a glance

### 2. **Add New Product** ➕
- Click "Add New Product" button
- Fill simple form
- Upload image
- Done!

### 3. **Edit Product** ✏️
- Click "Edit" on any product
- Update information
- Change image (optional)
- Save changes

### 4. **Delete Product** 🗑️
- Click "Delete" on any product
- Confirm deletion
- Product removed

### 5. **Search Products** 🔍
- Type product name in search box
- Results filter instantly
- Clear to see all

### 6. **Filter by Stock** 📊
- All Products
- Available (stock > 50)
- Low Stock (1-50)
- Out of Stock (0)

## 🎯 Step-by-Step Instructions

### **How to Add a Product**

1. Go to: http://127.0.0.1:8000/products/manage/
2. Click "➕ Add New Product"
3. Fill in:
   - Product Name
   - Description
   - Category
   - Price
   - Stock
   - Image
4. Click "Add Product"
5. ✅ Done!

---

### **How to Edit a Product**

1. Go to: http://127.0.0.1:8000/products/manage/
2. Find the product
3. Click "✏️ Edit"
4. Update fields
5. Click "Save Changes"
6. ✅ Done!

---

### **How to Delete a Product**

1. Go to: http://127.0.0.1:8000/products/manage/
2. Find the product
3. Click "🗑️ Delete"
4. Click "Yes, Delete" to confirm
5. ✅ Done!
