### Hover Effects
```css
- Card lifts up 8px on hover
- Image scales to 108% on hover
- Buttons lift 2px with shadow on hover
- Smooth 0.3s transitions
```

### Responsive Grid
```
- XL screens (1200px+): 4 products per row
- LG screens (992px+): 3 products per row
- MD screens (768px+): 2 products per row
- SM screens (576px+): 2 products per row
- Mobile (<576px): 1 product per row
```
