📸 Upload Real Product Images

Get exact product images like the laptop photo you showed me!

1 Open Unsplash (Free Images)

Unsplash has thousands of high-quality, free product photos.

Open Unsplash.com

2 Search for Product

Search for the exact product you need:

  • "laptop" - for laptop products
  • "smartphone" - for phone products
  • "bluetooth speaker" - for speakers
  • "headphones" - for headphones/earbuds
  • "jeans" - for denim jeans
  • "white t-shirt" - for t-shirts
  • "sneakers" - for shoes
  • "smartwatch" - for watches

3 Download Image

Click on an image you like, then click the green "Download free" button.

💡 Tip: Look for images with clean backgrounds (white or black) like the laptop image you showed me!

4 Open Django Admin

Go to your product management panel:

Open Admin Panel

Login: admin / admin123

5 Find Your Product

Scroll through the list or use the search box to find the product you want to update.

For example, search for "laptop" to find all laptop products.

6 Upload Image

1. Click on the product name to edit it

2. Scroll down to the "Image" field

3. Click "Choose File" or "Browse"

4. Select the image you downloaded from Unsplash

5. Click "Save" at the bottom

7 View Your Product

Go to your store and see the new image!

View Products

📝 Example: Adding Laptop Image

  1. Go to Unsplash.com
  2. Search "laptop"
  3. Find a nice laptop image (like the one you showed me)
  4. Download it
  5. Go to Admin Panel → Products
  6. Find "Laptop Backpack" or "Laptop Cooling Pad"
  7. Click to edit
  8. Upload the laptop image
  9. Save
  10. Done! ✅
