→ Go to: http://127.0.0.1:8000/admin/products/product/
→ Login: admin / admin123

STEP 5: Find Product
→ Search for product (e.g., "laptop")
→ Click on product name

STEP 6: Upload Image
→ Scroll to "Image" field
→ Click "Choose File"
→ Select downloaded image
→ Click "Save"

STEP 7: View Result
→ Go to: http://127.0.0.1:8000/products/
→ See your new product image!
