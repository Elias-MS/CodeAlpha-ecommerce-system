### **Add a Product**
1. Click "➕ Add New Product"
2. Fill in the form:
   - Product Name
   - Description
   - Category
   - Price
   - Stock
   - Image
3. Click "Add Product"
4. Done! ✅

### **Edit a Product**
1. Find the product
2. Click "✏️ Edit"
3. Update fields
4. Click "Save Changes"
5. Done! ✅

### **Delete a Product**
1. Find the product
2. Click "🗑️ Delete"
3. Confirm deletion
4. Done! ✅

### **Search Products**
1. Type in search box
2. Results filter instantly
3. Clear to see all

### **Filter by Stock**
1. Click filter button:
   - All Products
   - Available
   - Low Stock
   - Out of Stock
2. Products filter instantly
