## 💰 Currency Support Added!

✅ **Currency symbol displayed throughout the site**
✅ **Default: $ (US Dollar)**

### Change Currency:

Edit `ecommerce/settings.py`:

```python
CURRENCY_SYMBOL = '$'   # Change to: €, £, ₹, ¥
CURRENCY_CODE = 'USD'   # Change to: EUR, GBP, INR, JPY
```

**Examples:**
- Euro: `'€'` and `'EUR'`
- Pound: `'£'` and `'GBP'`
- Rupee: `'₹'` and `'INR'`
- Yen: `'¥'` and `'JPY'`
