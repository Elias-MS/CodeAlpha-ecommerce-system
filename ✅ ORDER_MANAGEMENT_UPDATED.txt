💡 WHY DELIVERED IS LOCKED:

✅ ORDER DELIVERED = FINAL STATUS
   - Customer received product
   - Transaction complete
   - Cannot go back to pending/processing
   - Prevents accidental status changes
   - Maintains order integrity

❌ Cannot Change To:
   - Pending (order already done)
   - Processing (order already done)
   - Shipped (order already delivered)
   - Cancelled (too late, already delivered)
