🔄 Product Active/Deactive Feature

Control Product Visibility with One Click!

💡 What It Does

The Active/Deactive feature lets you hide products from customers without deleting them. Perfect for expensive products, out-of-season items, or products waiting for restock. All product data is preserved and can be reactivated anytime!

⚖️ Active vs Inactive Products

✅ Active Product
  • 👁️ Visible to customers
  • 🛒 Can be purchased
  • 🔍 Shows in search
  • 📱 Shows on home page
  • 🏷️ Shows in categories
🚫 Inactive Product
  • 👁️ Hidden from customers
  • 🛒 Cannot be purchased
  • 🔍 Hidden from search
  • 📱 Hidden from home page
  • 🏷️ Hidden from categories

🚀 How to Use

1
Go to Manage Products page
2
Find the product you want to toggle
3
Click Activate or Deactivate button
4
Confirm the action - Done!

📋 When to Use

💰 Expensive Products
Hide products that are too expensive to sell right now. Reactivate when ready to sell at that price.
📦 Waiting for Stock
Hide out-of-stock products while waiting for supplier. Reactivate when stock arrives.
🌡️ Seasonal Items
Hide winter products in summer, summer products in winter. Keep your store relevant!
🧪 Testing Prices
Hide product, update price, reactivate. Test different prices without customer confusion.
🚀 Launch Control
Add products early, keep them inactive. Activate when ready to launch!
🔧 Maintenance
Hide products while updating descriptions, images, or details. Reactivate when done.
