## 🎯 OPTION 1: Use Free Stock Photo Services (RECOMMENDED)

### Best Free Image Sources:

1. **Unsplash** - https://unsplash.com/
   - High-quality, free product photos
   - No attribution required
   - Categories: Electronics, Fashion, Home, etc.

2. **Pexels** - https://www.pexels.com/
   - Free stock photos
   - Great for products
   - Easy to download

3. **Pixabay** - https://pixabay.com/
   - Free images
   - Commercial use allowed
   - Large collection

### How to Use:
1. Search for your product (e.g., "laptop", "jeans", "speaker")
2. Download the image
3. Rename to `product_[id].jpg`
4. Upload to `media/products/` folder
